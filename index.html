About QwikFix

Professional mobile mechanics you can trust

Our Story

QwikFix Mobile Mechanics was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: bring professional auto repair services directly to customers, saving them time and hassle.

What started as a single van operation has grown into a fleet of mobile service vehicles, covering the entire metropolitan area with qualified mechanics ready to help.

Our Approach

We believe automotive service should be transparent, convenient, and stress-free. All our mechanics are ASE certified, background-checked, and committed to excellent customer service.
